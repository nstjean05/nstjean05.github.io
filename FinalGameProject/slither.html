COSMIS — Mission Manual

About

COSMIS is a cosmic serpent survival game built with A-Frame for desktop and mobile VR. Guide your serpent through the nebula, devouring stardust and energy orbs to grow. Outmanoeuvre rival serpents and survive long enough to advance through the deeper sectors of the cosmos.

Installation

  1. No installation required — runs entirely in a modern web browser.
  2. Open this file in Chrome, Firefox, or Edge.
  3. For VR mode: enable Stereoscopic in Setup, then open on a mobile device and insert into a Google Cardboard-compatible headset.
  4. Tap the VR icon (bottom-right of the A-Frame scene) to enter stereoscopic view.

Controls — Desktop

  • A / D — Turn left / right
  • Mouse — Steer (move mouse left/right)
  • Space — Boost (costs mass — drops stardust behind you)

Controls — Mobile VR (Gaze)

  • Gaze left / right — Look left or right to steer your serpent in that direction
  • Gaze down — Tilt your head down to activate boost
  • No hand controllers required — fully gaze-driven

Gameplay

  • Fly through the nebula and collect glowing stardust orbs to grow your serpent.
  • Grow longer to grow wider — your mass increases with length.
  • Rival serpents die if their head collides with your body. You die if your head hits theirs.
  • Use the progress bar at the top to see how close you are to the next level.
  • Do not leave the nebula boundary — the void will destroy you.

Scoring

  • Score = current serpent length
  • Defeated serpents scatter stardust you can collect for a mass bonus
  • Kill counter tracked throughout your run

Levels

  • Level 1 — Nebula Cloud: Open arena, gentle rivals, abundant stardust.
  • Level 2 — Asteroid Drift: More rivals, slightly scarcer stardust.
  • Level 3 — Ion Belt: Faster rivals and a tighter survival window.
  • Level 4 — Rift Swarm: Heavier density and more pressure to boost smartly.
  • Level 5 — Nova Run: Large rivals, tighter space, harsher resource economy.
  • Level 6 — Void Crown: Final, extreme difficulty sector with the most aggressive encounters.
